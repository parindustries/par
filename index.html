    Services


    Cold Forging

    PAR Industries has in house cold forging machines which are appreciated for product's high tensile strength and long lasting life.

    These machines help us to make different types of fasterners in different sizes and shapes as per standard and also customer oriented.


    Hot Forging

    PAR Industries has in house manufacturing setup for hot forging bigger diameter bolts and nuts.

    From standard hex bolts to fully customized special shaped heads. We have the abilty to forge thousands of sizes and shapes with outstanding precision, dimensional accuracy, corrosion resistance and durable standards.


    Trop Facilities

    PAR Industries has Trop machines which enables us to make varieties of bar nuts from the range of 5/16, 3/8, 1/2, 5/8, 3/4, 7/16, 1 and 1.1/4.

    These bar nuts are used in many sectors. Trop machines enable us to make bar nuts as per customer drawings and specification as well.
